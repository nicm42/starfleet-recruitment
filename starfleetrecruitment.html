Entrance requirements

Entrance exam. This looks for qualities we expect in Starfleet officers: integrity, intelligence, courage, imagination and leadership qualities. You should expect the unexpected during the exam. The exam is open to all Federation citizens, and to non-Federation citizens with a letter of reference from a command level Starfleet officer.
